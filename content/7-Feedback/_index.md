After completing my internship at the First Cloud AI Journey program, I would like to share my personal thoughts and some small suggestions. I hope these insights will help the FCAJ team continue to improve and deliver even more value:

### Overall Impressions

**1. Work Environment**  
The work environment here is incredibly dynamic and open. The FCAJ team members are enthusiastic and always ready to "rescue" me when I run into errors, even outside of normal working hours. The workspace is comfortable and inspiring. However, organizing a few more small teambuilding activities or casual get-togethers would definitely help everyone bond and understand each other much better.

**2. Mentor and Team Admin Support**  
I am truly impressed by my mentor's approach: highly dedicated, explaining things thoroughly, and always creating an open space for questions. The admin team is also very attentive with paperwork and documentation. What I loved most is that my mentor always encouraged me to explore and debug on my own rather than just handing me the solutions, which helped me mature a lot technically.

**3. Relevance to My Major**  
The practical tasks here perfectly match my academic direction. Not only did I apply what I learned in school, but I was also exposed to brand-new technologies. As a result, I feel my foundational knowledge has been solidly reinforced.

**4. Opportunities for Personal Growth**  
This internship gave me more than just coding skills; it taught me teamwork, how to use project management tools, and workplace communication. The industry stories and real-world experiences shared by my mentor serve as valuable baggage for my future career path.

**5. Company Culture**  
The team spirit at FCAJ is fantastic. Everyone respects each other—working hard but also knowing how to have fun. During tight deadlines, regardless of who is staff or intern, everyone rolls up their sleeves to help. That made me feel like I truly belonged here.

**6. Internship Benefits**  
The company is very accommodating, offering a helpful allowance and flexible working hours. Additionally, the internal training sessions are an absolute "bargain" in terms of knowledge gained.

### Additional Thoughts
*   **Most satisfying aspect of the internship:** The dedication of my mentor and the "teaching how to fish" approach to guidance.
*   **Small suggestion for future cohorts:** Organize more off-the-clock social activities to strengthen bonding among members.
*   **Would you recommend this internship to friends?:** 100% yes! You get to work directly in your major while enjoying an excellent environment to upgrade yourself and clarify your career path.

### Suggestions
*   **Ideas to improve the experience:** Increase the frequency of networking and social activities between teams to boost collective spirit.
*   **Would you like to continue the journey?:** Absolutely! I really love the working atmosphere and the company culture.
*   **Final words:** A huge thank you to the FCAJ family for welcoming and supporting me so wholeheartedly, making my internship incredibly fulfilling and memorable.
