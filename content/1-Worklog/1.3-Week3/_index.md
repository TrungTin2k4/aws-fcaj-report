### Week 3 Objectives:
* Architect a secure, isolated virtual network environment.
* Launch a managed relational database in a protected subnet.
* Configure hybrid DNS resolution spanning on-premises and AWS.

* Architected a resilient Amazon VPC with logical separation between public-facing and internal resources.
